### Greener {#greener}

Greener is a lean and mean test result explorer.<br />
It is small (~27mb), easy to use, supports SQL-like query language and has plugins for various languages/frameworks.

Check out [main project repository](https://github.com/cephei8/greener) or [documentation](https://greener.cephei8.dev) for details.
